About us

Satoko Yen Design is a Japanese design consultancy based in the Bay Area. We bring a detail-oriented and innovative approach to our design process and work with a team of experienced consultants, including an award-winning contemporary architecture firm based in Japan. We pride ourselves on our serene and sophisticated style, and you can find our work in cities around the Bay Area, including in Alamo, Burlingame, Campbell, Danville, Fremont, Menlo Park, Mountain View, Palo Alto, San Fransisco, San Jose, and Sunnyvale.
